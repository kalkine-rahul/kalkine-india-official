We have a rocking story

Following the footsteps of the “father of value investing”, Benjamin Graham, Kalkine was founded with an aim of channelizing energies and enthusiasm towards the stock market into something that operates for the greater good.

The founding blocks, ethics and code of conduct were laid and our CEO, Mr. Kunal Sawhney started to put together people who he thought could understand his vision. From very humble beginnings, we took everyday as it came and still believe it to be day zero for us in terms of where we wish to reach.
