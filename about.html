About Us

Empowering Investors Globally with Research & Insights.

Following the footsteps of Benjamin Graham – the father of value investing, we channel our energies and enthusiasm into empowering investors with quality research, actionable insights, and timely recommendations.

Kalkine was founded with the vision to transform stock market passion into a platform that benefits investors at large. Led by our CEO, Mr. Kunal Sawhney, and guided by strong ethics, we’ve grown from humble beginnings into a global financial research firm with a loyal subscriber base built on trust and transparency.

Today, we are a strong and driven team of over 400+ professionals across India, Australia, the UK, and New Zealand, with expansion underway in Canada and the US. Our mission remains the same: to deliver reliable financial research that helps investors identify opportunities and grow with confidence.
